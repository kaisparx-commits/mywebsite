About the Artist

Early Life & Inspiration

Born and raised in Milan, I was surrounded by a rich tapestry of classical art and modern design. After moving to London to study Fine Arts, I began experimenting with urban photography, blending the structured forms of cityscapes with the fluid unpredictability of nature.

Artistic Process

My creative process usually begins with long walks through the city, capturing fleeting moments and architectural details. Back in the studio, these photographs become the foundation for my canvas works. I layer acrylics and digital overlays to build texture and depth.

Exhibitions

Over the past five years, my work has been featured in several independent galleries across Europe, including the 'Urban Whispers' solo exhibition. I have also collaborated with lifestyle brands to bring my art to everyday objects, inspiring this online store.

Vision

Ultimately, my goal is to create art that resonates on a personal level, offering a moment of stillness in a fast-paced world. Whether through a large-scale canvas or a simple studio mug, I hope my work brings a touch of inspiration to your daily life.
