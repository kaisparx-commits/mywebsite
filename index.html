Welcome to the digital gallery of Elena Rossi, an independent contemporary artist. My work explores the intersection of urban environments and natural landscapes, using mixed media to capture the essence of modern living.

Through a combination of photography, digital illustration, and traditional canvas work, I aim to evoke a sense of quiet introspection. Explore the portfolio to see my latest exhibitions, or visit the shop to purchase limited edition prints and merchandise.
